Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

To create an advertisement for your product on NewOLX, follow these simple steps:
  • Sign in to your NewOLX account or register if you're a new user.
  • Click on the "Post an Ad" / "Sell Your Item" button.
  • Fill in the required details about your product, including title, description, category, price, and upload images.
  • Review and confirm the information before posting your advertisement.

You can advertise a wide range of products on NewOLX, including electronics, fashion items, vehicles, real estate, services, and more. However, certain restricted items, such as illegal or prohibited goods, may not be allowed. Refer to our guidelines when posting your listings.

Posting advertisements on NewOLX is free for standard listings. However, there might be optional paid features or promotional tools available for a fee. We do not charge commission on successful transactions between buyers and sellers.

Interested buyers can contact you directly through the email address or phone number provided. You'll receive notifications and messages from potential buyers interested in your product, allowing you to communicate with only serious buyers.
